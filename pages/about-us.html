ABOUT OUR FISH RESTAURANT

Sea Garden - Sweden’s first 100% sustainable fish restaurant- is home to the #Oyster Happy Hour, best oyster happy hour deal across all our locations. In all our locations, we service creative seafood complete with warm hospitality. Global flavors and innovative preparations highlight our sustainable seafood, heritage meats and organic produce. For us sustainability of not a luxury, it is our moto. For business diners, special occasions, and dining out just-because, we are here to serve you a sustainable and memorable meal.
